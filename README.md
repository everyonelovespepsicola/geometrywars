# Geometry Wars

A fast-paced, neon-infused arcade twin-stick shooter built with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. Defend the grid against endless waves of geometric enemies!

## Features

- **Classic Twin-Stick Action:** Smooth and responsive movement and aiming mechanics.
- **Dynamic Grid:** A reactive background grid that warps and distorts from explosions and gravity wells.
- **Varied Enemy Types:** Fight against grunts, weavers, spinners, gravity wells, snake-like enemies, and more, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.
- **Boss Fights:** Challenging multi-phase bosses (Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald) spawn every 3 levels.
- **Power-ups & Progression:** Build your score streak for weapon spread and fire rate upgrades. Earn extra lives (every 15,000 pts) and bombs (every 30,000 pts).
- **Gamepad Support:** Full controller support for an authentic arcade experience.
- **Procedural Audio:** Synthesized sound effects generated dynamically using the Web Audio API.

## Controls

### Keyboard & Mouse
- **Move:** `W`, `A`, `S`, `D`
- **Aim & Shoot:** Mouse Movement & Left Click
- **Bomb:** `B`
- **Fullscreen:** `F`
- **Pause:** `ESC`

### Gamepad
- **Move:** Left Stick
- **Aim & Shoot:** Right Stick
- **Bomb:** Right Trigger (RT)
- **Pause:** Start Button

## Gameplay Tips

- Keep moving! Don't let yourself get cornered on the edges of the grid.
- Collect the purple orbs dropped by defeated enemies to boost your score.
- Use your bombs wisely when overwhelmed to clear the screen and warp the grid.
- Watch out for Gravity Wells—they'll pull you, the enemies, and the grid towards them!
